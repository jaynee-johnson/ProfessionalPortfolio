| [Routing Documentation](routing/routing.md) | <p>This was originally created in MadCap Flare. I converted it to Markdown for two reasons: 1) to demonstrate my proficiency in a docs-as-code approach, and 2) as a means to remove links that went outside of this section of the NICE CXone online help.</p><p>Despite routing being NICE's bread and butter, there was no documentation dedicated to routing for many years. Once I identified this gap, I pulled together documentation from all corners of the online help and copied it into a new Routing section. I utlized snippets to single-source the content so that the pieces could live both here and with their relevant applications.</p> <p>I did not write all of this documentation, but I did pull together the documentation from various sources, organized it in a cohesive way, and filled in the gaps.</p> <p>Sections I did write include Routing Attributes and most of the content for Dynamic Delivery.</p> |
| [Release Notes Archive](releaseNotesArchive/index.htm) | <p>I owned the release notes process and final output at NICE CXone for several years.
